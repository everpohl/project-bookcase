Size:    16×16 px

The side (book-spine) face of the warped bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are warped
plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with warped plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/warped_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #398382  light plank tone  (most common)
     #287067  light-mid tone
     #2E5F51  mid tone
     #3A8E8C  highlight
     #1F5752  mid-dark tone
     #1E4340  dark grain
     #113835  darkest grain

5. Visual character: muted teal/blue-green from the Nether — the only cool-toned wood
   in the game. The strips will be clearly non-wooden in feel, reading as alien or
   otherworldly.

When done, delete this file and commit warped_bookshelf.png in its place.
