The side (book-spine) face of the spruce bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are spruce
plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with spruce plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/spruce_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #82613A  light plank tone  (most common)
     #7A5A34  light-mid tone
     #70522E  mid tone
     #886539  highlight
     #614B2E  mid-dark tone
     #5A4424  dark grain
     #553A1F  darkest grain

5. Visual character: medium-dark warm brown, noticeably darker than oak. Richer and
   deeper, with a subtle reddish undertone in the grain.

When done, delete this file and commit spruce_bookshelf.png in its place.
