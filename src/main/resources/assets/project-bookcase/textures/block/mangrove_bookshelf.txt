The side (book-spine) face of the mangrove bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are mangrove
plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with mangrove plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/mangrove_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #7F4234  light plank tone  (most common)
     #773934  light-mid tone
     #753136  mid tone
     #8B4D3A  highlight
     #6F2A2D  mid-dark tone
     #642423  dark grain
     #5D1C1E  darkest grain

5. Visual character: deep reddish-brown with a distinct crimson tint — darker and
   redder than jungle, noticeably different from the standard earth-tone woods.

When done, delete this file and commit mangrove_bookshelf.png in its place.
