The side (book-spine) face of the crimson bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are crimson
plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with crimson plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/crimson_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #7E3A56  light plank tone  (most common)
     #6A344B  light-mid tone
     #5C3042  mid tone
     #863E5A  highlight
     #4B2737  mid-dark tone
     #442131  dark grain
     #3F1E2D  darkest grain

5. Visual character: deep purple-crimson from the Nether. Unlike any overworld wood —
   the strips will be dark and purplish-red, reading as clearly supernatural.

When done, delete this file and commit crimson_bookshelf.png in its place.
