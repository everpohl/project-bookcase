The side (book-spine) face of the bamboo bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are bamboo
plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with bamboo plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/bamboo_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #C8B14D  light plank tone  (most common)
     #E3CC6A  lighter accent
     #D9C25E  light-mid tone
     #EFD97E  highlight
     #D3BB50  mid tone
     #C2B54E  mid-dark tone
     #BBA84C  dark tone
     #B7AB49  darker tone
     #998741  dark grain
     #907E3A  darkest grain

5. Visual character: warm golden-yellow, distinctly yellow-green compared to all other
   wood types. The strips will be bright and straw-like, unlike any tree-based plank.
   Bamboo planks also have a slightly busier, more varied palette (10 colors vs 7).

When done, delete this file and commit bamboo_bookshelf.png in its place.
