The side (book-spine) face of the acacia bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are acacia
plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with acacia plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/acacia_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #BA6337  light plank tone  (most common)
     #AD5D32  light-mid tone
     #A05630  mid tone
     #C26D3F  highlight
     #99502B  mid-dark tone
     #8F4C2A  dark grain
     #884728  darkest grain

5. Visual character: distinctive orange-red — the most saturated of all wood types.
   The plank strips will stand out strongly against the book spines.

When done, delete this file and commit acacia_bookshelf.png in its place.
