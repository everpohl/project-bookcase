The side (book-spine) face of the birch bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are birch
plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with birch plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/birch_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #D7C185  light plank tone  (most common)
     #C8B77A  light-mid tone
     #B8A875  mid tone
     #D7CB8D  highlight
     #AE9F76  mid-dark tone
     #A59467  dark grain
     #9E8B61  darkest grain

5. Visual character: pale creamy yellow — the lightest of the overworld wood types.
   The grain is subtle; the overall impression is bright and almost straw-colored.

When done, delete this file and commit birch_bookshelf.png in its place.
