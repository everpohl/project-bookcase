The side (book-spine) face of the dark oak bookshelf. Rows 0–1 and 14–15 are dark
oak plank strips; rows 2–13 are book spines shared across all wood variants.

--- HOW TO CREATE ---

1. Open vanilla bookshelf.png (minecraft:block/bookshelf) as your starting point.
   Extract it from the Minecraft jar — see TEXTURES.md for the unzip command.

2. Recolor ONLY these rows — replace the oak plank tones with dark oak plank tones:
     Row  0  (top strip, lighter tone)
     Row  1  (top strip, dark grain line)
     Row 14  (bottom strip, mid tone)
     Row 15  (bottom strip, lighter tone)

3. Leave rows 2–13 (book spines) unchanged.

4. Reference palette — minecraft:block/dark_oak_planks (from 26.1.2.jar)
     #4F3218  light plank tone  (most common)
     #492F17  light-mid tone
     #3E2912  mid tone
     #53381A  highlight
     #3A2411  mid-dark tone
     #301E0E  dark grain
     #291A0C  darkest grain

5. Visual character: very dark brown, nearly black-brown — the darkest overworld wood.
   The plank strips will be almost black at the edges, contrasting sharply with the
   colorful book spines.

When done, delete this file and commit dark_oak_bookshelf.png in its place.
